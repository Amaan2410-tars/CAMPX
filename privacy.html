Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-07
This is the privacy policy for CampX (web), effective from the date above.

1) Who we are

CampX ("we", "us") provides a student-only social platform. Contact: ghoriamaan01@gmail.com.

2) Data we collect

  • Account: name, email, college, program/year, profile details.
  • Content: posts, comments, reactions, messages and related metadata.

3) How we use data

4) Verification and founder approval

For launch, student verification may require manual approval by a founder/admin. We may review limited account information to accept or reject access. We do not sell personal information.

5) Sharing

  • Vendors: hosting, analytics (if enabled), email/SMS/push providers, Razorpay for payments.

6) Retention

We keep data as long as needed for the service and legal obligations. You may request deletion subject to required retention for fraud/security/legal reasons.

7) Security

We use access controls, encryption in transit, and database security rules. No system is 100% secure; please report issues to ghoriamaan01@gmail.com.

8) Your choices

  • Update your profile and settings in the app.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion via support.

9) Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post updates here with a new effective date.
