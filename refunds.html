Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-07
Replace placeholders before go-live.

1) Subscriptions

Paid plans (e.g. Pro/Plus) may be billed monthly or annually. You can cancel anytime from the Billing page. Unless otherwise stated, cancellation stops renewal at the end of the current billing period.

2) Refunds

  • Default policy: subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period starts, except where required by law or in cases of duplicate/erroneous charges.
  • Duplicate charge: report within 7 days and we’ll review and process a refund if confirmed.

3) How to request a refund

Email support@campx.social with your account email, payment reference/receipt, and the reason. We may request additional verification to prevent fraud.

4) Refund timelines

If approved, refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Timelines vary by bank/payment method, typically 5-10 business days.

5) Contact

For billing help, see Contact.
